Garlic Bread

Description

Perfectly toasted garlic bread that can be done in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mince garlic: Take garlic and dice or grind them into smaller pieces and set aside.
  2. Slice butter: Slice a slab of butter and add into a bowl for mixing. Set the butter at room temperature.
  3. Add salt, milk and olive oil: Once the butter has reached room temperature, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of milk, and a pinch of salt. Add in the minced garlic and mix well.
  4. Spread mixture: Spread the mixture onto the bread and then cook in the oven until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Dish it up and serve hot!
