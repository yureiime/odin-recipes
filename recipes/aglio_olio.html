Aglio Olio

Description

Easy to make aglio olio, inspired by my girlfriend.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Pasta of choice (speghetti, linguine, fuscili etc)
  • Meat of choice (minced beef would be perfect)
  • Vegetable of choice
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mince garlic: Take garlic and dice or grind them into smaller pieces and set aside.
  2. Cook pasta: Salt the pasta water generously and cook the pasta until it is al dente.
  3. Stir fry meat: Add olive oil into a hot pan, add in the garlic and then stir fry the meat into the pan when the garlic starts to brown.
  4. Add pasta: Add the pasta into the pan and stir fry, adding condiments like pepper to spice up the taste. Garnish for aesthetic purposes.
  5. Serve: Dish it up and serve hot!
